Who we are

Ruggedly reliable air compressors manufactured for individuals who want their equipment to work as hard as they do. At Knight our goals are to manufacture simple to operate, maintain and service air compressors. We take out all the extra cost of the middle man and give you the opportunity to deal directly with the manufacturer. The units ship straight from our manufacturing facility to your door. Our facility is strategically located in Texas so that almost ½ of the US population is just a 1-day shipping point away.

Our air compressors are assembled with heavy duty components that we have tested to ensure they meet our exact standards. If you are tired of going to the store to get a new air compressor every 1 -3 years and want a heavy-duty quality product, then Knight air compressors are what you are looking for. Knight Air Compressors for folks who want to get the job done.
